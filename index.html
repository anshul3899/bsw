About Us

BSW “Board for Student Welfare” consists of student representatives from each hostel and a few faculty members. This board has been constituted to help the students in the hour of need. BSW gives financial help, informative sessions, counselling and looks after other modes of student welfare.

Objective

The permanent committees of BSW are:

Operations

The Operations Commitee of BSW executes welfare activities in the institute to benefit the student community. It also provides assistance (financial and in kind) to the students in need. Every hostel has two BSW Representatives who carries out such welfare activities along with a special focus on guiding and helping the freshers. Our activities include smooth induction of the orientation process, setting up of various camps such as cycle camp, book camp, pan card camp etc., conducting informative sessions such as foreign exchange, foreign intern, CAT etc, organising Speranza (annual youth festival) and carrying out various initiatives for student welfare throughout the year. It also looks over SCOOPS, SCS and STIC which is an integral part of BSW.

Mentorship

The Mentorship Committee of BSW executes & monitors all mentorship initiatives & activities for IIT Delhi students. These include freshers' orientation, mentorship of freshmen students , student-alumni mentorship, academic mentorship, language mentorship, counseling services, etc. On the commencement of one's IITD life, every individual is provided a mentor who is a trained & experienced senior student. The mentor is responsible for making the student capable of taking informed decisions in academic, extra-curricular & personal fronts by sharing unbiased knowledge & experiences. Mentors are also expected to contribute their efforts in other mentorship initiatives.
